§ 01

About the Collective

We are a collective of researchers and engineers dedicated to pushing the boundaries of Large Language Models.

Here at OpenDih, we are building the world's first collective-persona LLMs. Our models are trained on the lived experience and technical lore of thousands of developers, creating an AI that feels less like a machine and more like a room full of elite engineers.

The collective-persona approach represents a fundamental departure from standard training methodology — encoding not just knowledge, but epistemic character.
